Branded IDs

In the quiet of the runtime, schemas hold their ground,
A prefix and a cuid2, collision-free and sound.
usr_, ord_, ses_ — small sigils of intent,
Each string a promise kept, each type a covenant.

Effect threads the needle, validation in its wake,
No accidental crossing of the boundaries we make.
So here in fold's small garden of packages and code,
A poem for the branded IDs that lighten every load.
