# A Session's Song

One session minted, root agent born,
a working directory, a system-prompt at dawn.
Durable rows are appended, kept and true,
while ephemeral deltas stream and pass on through.

Text and reasoning, fragments in flight,
tool-progress whispers that vanish from sight.
The log remembers what the moment forgot,
a merged stream folding the kept and the not.

Publish to all who subscribe and attend,
a live-only current with no replay to send.
So the contract holds, and the spine stays clean:
what is written endures, what is felt goes unseen.
